# 06 — Routines Reference

A routine is a recurring study habit assigned to a child (PRD, §6.2). This document records the
default routines provisioned with each child profile and the rules that govern cadence, stars, and
streaks in the current build.

## Default routines

Creating a child profile provisions the following three routines for that child (PRD, §6.2;
UI: Today). The titles, icons, cadences, and star rewards below are the values delivered by the
current build.

| Routine | Icon | Cadence | Stars awarded |
|---------|------|---------|---------------|
| Read for 20 minutes | 📖 | Daily | 15 |
| Math facts — 10 questions | ➗ | Weekdays | 15 |
| Free-write for 5 minutes | ✍️ | Daily | 10 |

## Cadence

The cadence determines the days on which a routine is due and therefore appears in the plan:

- **Daily**: the routine is due every day.
- **Weekdays**: the routine is due Monday through Friday.

A routine appears in **Today's plan** only on a day on which it is due.

## Stars

A star is the unit of reward (PRD, §6.2). Completing a routine awards the number of stars stated
for that routine, and the award is added to the **stars today** count on the plan (UI: Today). The
star values for the default routines are listed in the table above.

## Completion and streaks

The following rules govern completion in the current build (UI: Today; PRD, §6.2):

- A routine is completed at most once per day. After completion its check control is disabled.
- The streak is the count of consecutive days on which the child completes routine activity. It
  reads one day on the first day of activity and advances on each successive active day.
- A missed day is not penalized; stars already earned are retained (PRD, §6.2).

## Customizing routines

The current build provisions the three default routines and supports their completion. Authoring,
editing, or removing routines through the interface is not yet available. Routine templates by
grade band and parent-configurable routines are specified in the requirements (PRD, §6.2) and are
**[Planned]**. Until then, the three default routines constitute the child's plan.
