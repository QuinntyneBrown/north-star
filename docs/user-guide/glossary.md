# Glossary

This glossary is the single source of truth for the terms used in the North Star user guide. Each
entry gives a single-sentence definition, an availability tag, and a citation. Where this glossary
and intuition disagree, the glossary prevails.

**Account owner**: the adult who creates and owns the family account and holds the Owner role.
**[Available]** (PRD, §6.1).

**Artifact**: a photo, file, link, or reflection attached to a project as evidence of work.
**[Planned: M3]** (PRD, §6.3).

**Availability tag**: a marker indicating whether a feature is implemented (**[Available]**) or
specified but not yet implemented (**[Planned]**) in the current build. (This guide.)

**Child**: the daily user of the application, signed in with a login handle and a personal
identification number rather than an email address. **[Available]** (PRD, §6.1).

**Child profile**: the record for one child, holding the first name, grade, optional interests,
login handle, and personal identification number. **[Available]** (PRD, §6.1).

**Daily loop**: the child's recurring practice of opening the plan, completing due routines, and
receiving the reward. **[Available]** (PRD, §5, §6.2).

**Family account**: the single account, owned by a parent, under which all child profiles and family
data reside. **[Available]** (PRD, §6.1).

**Grade**: the child's school grade, recorded as an integer from 1 to 12. **[Available]**
(UI: Add child; PRD, §6.1).

**Login handle**: the public identifier of at least three characters that a child uses to sign in.
**[Available]** (UI: Add child).

**Mentor**: a tutor, coach, or relative invited with limited access to encourage and verify
activities. **[Planned]** (PRD, §4, §11).

**Milestone**: a numbered increment (M0 through M7) of the development plan that delivers a defined
set of capabilities. (README; PRD, §11).

**Operator**: the internal administrator who manages the scholarship data pipeline and content
library. (PRD, §4).

**Opportunity**: a scholarship, contest, grant, program, or award held in the catalog and matched to
a child. **[Planned: M5]** (PRD, §6.5, §7).

**Parent**: the account owner in their guiding capacity; the term used for the adult participant
throughout this guide. **[Available]** (PRD, §4, §6.1).

**Personal identification number (PIN)**: the four-to-six-digit secret a child uses to sign in
alongside the login handle. **[Available]** (UI: Add child, Child login).

**Portfolio**: the timeline of a child's projects and interests that becomes admissions evidence.
**[Planned: M3]** (PRD, §6.3).

**Routine**: a recurring study habit assigned to a child, such as reading for twenty minutes.
**[Available]** (PRD, §6.2).

**Routine cadence**: the schedule on which a routine is due — *Daily* (every day) or *Weekdays*
(Monday through Friday). **[Available]** (UI: Today; PRD, §6.2).

**Star**: the unit of reward awarded when a child completes a routine. **[Available]** (PRD, §6.2;
UI: Today).

**Streak**: the count of consecutive days on which the child completes routine activity.
**[Available]** (PRD, §6.2; UI: Today).

**Today's plan**: the set of routines due for a child on the current day, shown at
`/children/{childId}/today`. **[Available]** (UI: Today; PRD, §6.2).
