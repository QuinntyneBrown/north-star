# 02 — Roles and Vocabulary

This guide names participants by their defined roles and uses a controlled set of terms. The
[Glossary](glossary.md) holds the complete definitions; this document describes the roles a parent
encounters in the current build and the terms most relevant to a first session.

## Roles

**Account owner (the parent)**: the adult who creates and owns the family account and who holds the
Owner role (PRD, §6.1). The account owner registers the family, creates child profiles, configures
access, and is the only role permitted to reach parent-only areas. **[Available]**

**Child**: the daily user of the application, signed in with a login handle and a personal
identification number rather than an email address (PRD, §6.1). The child completes the daily plan
and may not reach parent-only areas such as settings or scholarship approvals (PRD, §6.1).
**[Available]**

**Mentor**: a tutor, coach, or relative invited with limited access to encourage and verify
activities (PRD, §4, §6.1). **[Planned: M*later*]** — the role is specified but not yet operable
(PRD, §11, v2).

**Operator**: the internal administrator who manages the scholarship data pipeline and content
library (PRD, §4). The Operator role is not part of a family's use of the application and is out of
scope for this guide.

## Access boundary

The access boundary between the parent and the child is a normative property of the current build:

- The parent **shall** authenticate with an email address and a password of at least eight
  characters (PRD, §6.1; UI: Register).
- The child **shall** authenticate with a login handle and a personal identification number of four
  to six digits (PRD, §6.1; UI: Child login).
- The child **shall not** be able to reach parent-only areas; parent-only areas include settings,
  scholarship approvals, and account administration (PRD, §6.1).

## Core terms used in a first session

The following terms appear throughout the getting-started procedure. Each is defined in full in the
[Glossary](glossary.md).

- **Family account**: the single account, owned by a parent, under which all child profiles and
  family data reside (PRD, §6.1).
- **Child profile**: the record for one child, holding the grade, optional interests, login handle,
  and personal identification number (PRD, §6.1).
- **Login handle**: the public identifier a child uses to sign in, of at least three characters
  (UI: Add child).
- **Personal identification number (PIN)**: the four-to-six-digit secret a child uses to sign in
  alongside the login handle (UI: Add child, Child login).
- **Routine**: a recurring study habit assigned to a child, such as reading for twenty minutes
  (PRD, §6.2).
- **Today's plan**: the child's set of routines due on the current day (PRD, §6.2; UI: Today).
- **Star**: a unit of reward awarded when the child completes a routine (PRD, §6.2; UI: Today).
- **Streak**: the count of consecutive days on which the child has completed routine activity
  (PRD, §6.2; UI: Today).
